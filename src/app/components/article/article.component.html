Shop, play and dine at Egyp's best malls


Mon, 28 December 2020


Egypt is a shopper's dream and with a multitude of malls boasting fun
attractions, there's much to explore.

1

Mall of Egypt.


Luxury beckons at the Mall of the Egypt, where a wide range of couture and street-style fashion awaits. Highlights include the tech-focused Apple boutique, department stores like Harvey Nichols, Marks & Spencer, and Debenhams, as well as the exclusive edit of designers at the ultra-chic Fashion Dome. After a long day of shopping, savour a bowl of Cairo's favourite dumplings at Din Tai Fung, or sip on a warm cup of coffee at hip café, Common Grounds. Alternatively, enjoy sloping down the ice-clad hills at Ski Dubai, Dubai's indoor skiing arena, or let the kids loose at Magic Planet.
